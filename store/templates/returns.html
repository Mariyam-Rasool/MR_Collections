Returns & Exchanges

Returns

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 10 days of the delivery date for a full refund or exchange. Please ensure that the item is in its original condition and packaging, with all tags attached.

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at mrcollections.store@gmail.com to request a return authorization.
  2. Once your return request is approved, carefully package the item and include the return authorization number inside the package.
  3. Ship the item back to us using a trackable shipping service. The return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer.
  4. Once we receive your returned item and verify its condition, we will process your refund or exchange within 30 business days.
Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or style, please contact our customer service team at mrcollections.store@gmail.com to request an exchange authorization. Follow the same steps outlined above for initiating a return, and indicate the desired exchange item in your communication with our team.

Once we receive your returned item and verify its condition, we will process the exchange and ship out the new item to you within 15 business days.

Additional Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our returns and exchanges process, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team. We are here to assist you and ensure that your shopping experience with us is enjoyable and hassle-free.
