Shipping and Handling

Domestic Shipping

We are pleased to offer reliable shipping services across Pakistan. All orders are processed and shipped within 2-3 business days. Delivery times may vary depending on your location:

  • Major Cities: 2-3 business days
  • Other Areas: 4-5 business days

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on the weight and destination of your order.

International Shipping

Currently, we do not offer international shipping. Please check back in the future for updates on our shipping policies. If you have any further questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to our customer support team for assistance.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order through our shipping partner's website.

Handling Time

We take great care in handling and packaging your order to ensure it arrives in perfect condition. Please allow 2-3 business days for us to process and prepare your order for shipment.

Customs and Duties

All applicable customs and import duties, fees, and taxes are the responsibility of the customer. We are not responsible for any delays caused by customs processes.

Shipping Issues

If you encounter any issues with your shipment, please contact our customer service team at mrcollections.store@gmail.com. We are here to assist you with any concerns or questions you may have.

Returns and Exchanges

Please refer to our Returns Policy page for detailed information on how to return or exchange an item. If you have any further questions regarding our return process, don't hesitate to contact our customer support team for assistance. We're here to help!
